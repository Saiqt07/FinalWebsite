1

Notification Cleanse

Eliminate digital interruptions by turning off non-essential notifications that constantly demand your attention.

Today's Tasks:

  • Disable all social media notifications
  • Turn off email alerts on your phone
  • Keep only essential app notifications (messages, calendar)
  • Put your phone on silent during focused work periods
  • Schedule 3 specific times to check messages/emails

Reducing notifications helps regain focus and reduce stress

Reflection:

How did it feel to not be constantly interrupted? What did you do with the extra mental space?

2

Morning Unplug

Start your day with intention by avoiding digital devices for the first hour after waking.

Today's Tasks:

  • Keep your phone on airplane mode for the first 30-60 minutes
  • Establish a non-digital morning routine (journaling, stretching)
  • Don't check email/social media until after breakfast
  • Write down your top 3 priorities before looking at your phone
  • Enjoy your morning beverage without screens
3

Mealtime Mindfulness

Reclaim mealtimes as opportunities for connection and mindful eating without digital distractions.

Action Steps:

  • No phones at the table during meals
  • Turn off the TV during meals
  • Practice mindful eating - notice flavors and textures
  • If eating alone, enjoy the solitude instead of scrolling
  • Share one thing you're grateful for during shared meals
4

Social Media Timeout

Take a full day off from social media platforms to break the habit of constant checking and comparison.

Action Steps:

  • Delete social media apps from your phone for the day
  • Use browser blockers if needed to prevent access
  • Notice when you instinctively reach for social media
  • Replace social media time with a meaningful activity
  • Journal about how you feel without social media access
5

Digital Sunset

Implement an evening digital curfew to improve sleep quality and allow your mind to unwind.

Action Steps:

  • No screens 1 hour before bedtime
  • Enable night mode/blue light filter in the evening
  • Charge devices outside the bedroom
  • Replace late-night scrolling with reading or relaxation
  • Practice a brief gratitude reflection before sleep
6

Single-Tasking Day

Practice focused attention by doing one thing at a time without digital multitasking.

Action Steps:

  • Close all unnecessary browser tabs and apps
  • Work in 25-minute focused intervals (Pomodoro technique)
  • Put your phone away during conversations
  • No background TV while working/studying
  • Notice how your concentration improves
7

Full Digital Detox

Experience a full day without non-essential digital technology to appreciate life beyond screens.

Action Steps:

  • No social media, email, or web browsing
  • Use phone only for calls/texts (if essential)
  • Engage in analog activities (reading physical books, writing by hand)
  • Spend time in nature without documenting it
  • Connect with people face-to-face

A full digital detox day helps reset your relationship with technology
