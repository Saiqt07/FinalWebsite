The "No Phone After 9 PM" Challenge

Reclaim Your Evenings

Disconnecting in the evening leads to better sleep and relaxation

Challenge Overview

This 14-day challenge helps you establish healthy boundaries with your phone in the evening. By avoiding phone use after 9 PM, you'll improve sleep quality, reduce eye strain, and create space for more meaningful evening activities.

How It Works

  1. Days 1-3: Awareness phase - Track your evening phone usage
  2. Days 4-10: Implementation phase - No phone after 9 PM
  3. Days 11-14: Reflection phase - Notice improvements in sleep and relaxation

Evening Alternatives

  • Read a physical book or magazine
  • Practice meditation or gentle stretching
  • Have device-free conversations with family
  • Journal your thoughts on paper
  • Listen to calming music or podcasts

Benefits

  • Improved sleep quality and duration
  • Reduced exposure to blue light before bed
  • More meaningful connections with loved ones
  • Increased time for relaxation and self-care
  • Better work-life balance

Implementation Tips

  • Set a phone bedtime alarm for 8:45 PM as a reminder
  • Charge your phone outside the bedroom
  • Use an old-school alarm clock instead of your phone
  • Enable "Do Not Disturb" mode automatically at 9 PM
  • Prepare alternative activities in advance
