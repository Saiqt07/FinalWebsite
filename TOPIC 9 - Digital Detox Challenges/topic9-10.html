Group Digital Detox Challenges

Community support enhances commitment to digital wellness goals

30-Day Community Challenge

Join our monthly community detox challenge where participants support each other in reducing screen time and improving digital habits. Group accountability increases success rates by 65% compared to solo efforts.

How It Works

  1. Week 1: Baseline tracking and goal setting
  2. Week 2: Implementation with daily check-ins
  3. Week 3: Mid-challenge evaluation
  4. Week 4: Final push and reflection

Accountability Systems

Buddy System

  • Pair with an accountability partner
  • Daily check-ins via voice message (no texting)
  • Weekly video call progress reviews
  • Shared goal tracking spreadsheet

Community Features

  • Private online discussion forum
  • Weekly live video support sessions
  • Progress leaderboard (opt-in)
  • Monthly virtual celebration events
